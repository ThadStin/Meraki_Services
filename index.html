This is what we do

Meraki Services is a leading consultant and management firm specializing in mental health and substance abuse treatment programs throughout the United States. We assist organizations in achieving their goals in the areas of accreditation, quality, financial, and operations. Meraki Services has multidisciplinary expertise in navigating all projects from de novo to service line expansion to day-to-day operations oversight. Meraki Services also assists the individual consumer seeking bespoke behavioral healthcare treatment options that incorporate both traditional and nontraditional treatment modalities.

This is how we should do it

Mission - To increase individual access to quality behavioral healthcare options that integrate traditional and nontraditional treatment options.


Core Values
Innovation - We believe that the manner in which behavioral healthcare services are delivered should be constantly evolving to meet the needs of all people accessing care.
Disruption - We align ourselves with people, organizations and technologies that replace status quo behavioral healthcare delivery models and produces something more impactful and worthwhile
Bespoken Care - We acknowledge that meeting an individual’s maximum mental wellness cannot be completely anticipated or met in a primary treatment environment; because of this we place equal focus on identifying secondary and tertiary treatment solutions for those seeking our services.

Vision - An informed and empowered population with knowledge and access to resources to obtain mental wellness.
